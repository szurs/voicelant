Voicelant uses products Dialogic, offering a wide range of computer telephony boards that are connected both to the analog lines, and the digital trunks.

 

Analog Boards can have 2 (D/21H, ProLine/2V, D/21D), 4(D/4PCI, D/4PCIU, D/41E, D/41EPCI, D/41JCT, D/41ESC, D/41H, D/42, Dialog/4), 12(D/120JCT) and 16(D/160S) channels. In addition to a single computer can be set up to 8 boards. Ie Voicelant based on the analog boards installed in one computer can serve up to 64 phone calls simultaneously.

 

Digital Boards Dialogic (D/240, D/480, D/300, D/600, DM / V, etc.) are from 1 to 4 interfaces E1 (30 channels) или T1 (24 channels), can be installed by several in one computer, and supports various types of signalization - R2, ISDN PRI, SS7. On such boards, installed in one computer, Voicelant can serve up to 960 channels (32 trunks E1)!

 

For advanced configurations Voicelant allows to create a system on multiple computers with a dedicated database servers. Such systems can serve a large number of calls, and easily scalable. In addition, Voicelant allows to create a distributed systems, where the IVR is in several cities with centralized logic and database.
