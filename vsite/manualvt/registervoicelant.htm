Software Voicelant is not free.

 

To evaluate the software Voicelant you can download a trial version that runs 30 days, during which you can meet with all the features of the system. It has some limitations compared to a working version, for example, the number of calls for each channel is not more than 10, then you must restart the program.

 

If you decide to use Voicelant you must determine the number of licenses and to purchase a license key, then all restrictions will be lifted.

 

After 30 days the Voicelant cannot be used.        

 

You can order the software on our site Voicelant www.voicelant.com

Registration procedure:

 

·Following the decision to purchase a license for Voicelant you can make an order for registration Voicelant on our website with your details, number of channels (licenses) and wishes for the purchase. We'll send you an invoice for payment. Мы вышлем Вам счет на оплату.
· If you need a contract, you can download a copy of the contract, fill it out, sign, seal and send to us by mail. We guarantee a refund of your copy.
· After payment of the software Voicelant we will send you by email registration key within 3 days, which we generate on the serial number of your board Dialogic.
· If you purchase a system, consisting of boards and software Voicelant, after the payment we will send you a package with the product within 30 days, which already has a license key and after installation on your computer is ready to work.
