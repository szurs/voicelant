Tab <System> - setting system parameters.

· <GlobalCall proticol> - choice of protocol for the channels of system (SS7, ISDN - digital, pdk_na_an_io - analog, you can also enter a different protocol) ;
· <Telephony interface> - the choice of network telephone interface (analog, E1,T1);
· <Number of channels> - the maximum number of channels in the system;
· <Type voice files> - choice of the main types of voice files (WAV, VOX);
· <Direction of channels> - choosing the direction of the channel: IN – serves only incoming calls, OUT – serves only to outgoing calls, INOUT  - serves the incoming and outgoing calls, NOT – does not work;
· <Board DM3> - indicates if the signal channel (usually 16), can be used as a voice (for digital systems with ignalizatsiey SS7) if the card type used DM3 DM3 (only for digital systems with interfaces E1 , T1 );
· <Signal channel as voice> - indicates if the signal channel, can be used as a voice (for digital systems with signalization SS7)
