 Solutions
Voicelant offers solutions for the creation of computer telephony applications in various fields of human activity:

 Office
Autosecretary, voice / fax mail, universal mail (unified messaging), notification, autoattendant;

 Operators and content-providers
Advanced IVR, voice / fax mail, notification, fax-server, paid and free-of-charge autoinformers, call-center, billing system;

 Bank
Intellectual answering machine, inquiries on accounts, payment system, notification about payments, loan conditions;

 Insurance
Advertising services, registration of insurance claims, call the insurance agent, call-center;

 Trade
Order products, account status, inventory, advertising, payments over the phone;

 Medicine
Inquiries about availability of drugs; tips on health, diet; work schedule of doctors; write to the reception; analysis; diagnosis on the phone; a reminder of the procedures;

 Exchange
Quotes of securities, currency exchange, tele-trading;

 Education
Information for entering higher education, examination results, timetables, testing, tutoring, learning of foreign languages;

 Transportation
Timetables of trains, planes, buses; information about availability and booking of tickets re-routing; dispatch service;

 Tourism
Advertising travel, last minute trips, booking;

 Taxes
Services to taxpayers, tax rates, changes in legislation, a reminder of the tax payments;

 State Institutions
General information, schedule of work, receiving complaints and suggestions, write to the reception, hot lines;

 City Information
Information urban services: water, electricity, gas, heat supply; emergency and dispatch services, transport, call-center;

 Mass media
Televoting; make a request for radio, TV; reception announcements; hot lines;

 Technical Support
Answers to questions, receiving applications, information about the status of an order, call-center;
