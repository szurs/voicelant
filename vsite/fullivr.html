 Voicelant-IVR: information voice portal

Voicelant-IVR is ready for use software product which contains all necessary for creation a comprehensive information voice portal.
Voicelant-IVR combines the functions of Voice and Fax mail (VoiceMail + Fax), the extended IVR, universal messaging system (Unified Messaging), the server automatic outbound voice notification (AutoDialer), notifications via SMS and EMail, Call Center and others.

Voicelant-IVR has the ability to integrate with various databases, other programs, applications, Internet (EMail, HTTP, FTP), speech synthesis (TTS), the creation and management services through a WEB-interface, etc. etc.

Voicelant-IVR implements logging of access to services, prepares data on pricing of services, gives various statistical information.

Voicelant-IVR is connected both to the analog telephone lines, and digital interfaces E1 (T1).

When connected to a digital interface number of channels can be from 30 to 240 per server (1-8 E1) and is defined by the customer. Signaling Type - ISDN PRI, SS7. System is easily expanded by installing additional servers.

Identification numbers caused by services performed on the last four digits of the dialed number. Identification of the calling party based on the data contained in the information element Calling Party Number message SETUP (ISDN) or the IAM (SS7).

When connected to an analog telephone lines the number of channels is determined by the customer and may be 4, 8, 12 or 16 channels (or more, but then better to connect to a digital interface).

Identification numbers caused by services performed on the last four digits of telephone numbers from which comes the call, or, if appointed as the serial number on the DTMF-tone. Identification of the calling party is determined by the signals CallerID (if the station offers those opportunities).


The system of providing services Voicelant-IVR.

Services are 4-digit numbers, which allows to introduce up to 10000 different services.

Each service Voicelant-IVR is working on a scenario, which defines the functions of specific services. Scenario can be easily modified by using tools Voicelant.

All services are divided into three main types:

VoiceMail Box + Fax (VMB).

Classical voice fax mailbox VMB (VoiceMail + Fax) works as follows: the recording is available for any caller to the number of services, listening to - only for the owner who knows the password.

You can record a voice or facsimile messages.

To listen to recorded messages VMB owner should dial the phone number on your voicemail box and enter the password. After that, the owner can work with your VMB with a standard set of control codes.

The owner has the opportunity at any time:

  • gain access to own VMB from any phone;
  • listen received messages "leaf over" them from the start or end by pressing keys on the phone (2-top, 4-previous, 5-repetition of the message, 6-the next, 8-last). The command can be entered, without waiting for the end of the speech communication. Fax, you can take on the fax machine, after the phrase "fax-message. Click the "START";
  • remove unwanted messages;
  • record voice and fax messages;
  • change the title VMB, which is always pronounced with the call in VMB and can be recorded only by the owner. Typically, the header contains a greeting the owner, name of firm, etc.;
  • turned on and off VMB;
  • establish a new password VMB;
  • change the mode of access to VMB - around the clock or at a specified time;
  • obtain information on the number of calls to VMB;
  • receive SMS on mobile phone about incoming message in VMB with phone number of subscriber;
  • receive notification about incoming messages in VMB on e-mail address;
  • get itself received the message by e-mail in a format that allows to listen to speech (voice) or view the fax message on a computer;
  • send messages to other VMB, VAI or email address;
  • deliver voice and fax messages on given phone numbers;
  • repeal has not yet made the operation of delivery;
  • verify the result of the delivery of voice and fax messages;
  • turn on the mode of communication attributes which when listening to messages will pronounce the date, time and phone number from which it was received;
  • mode "free hand" in which the listening you do not need to press control buttons. The next message will be pronounced automatically;
  • receive help on the rules of use VMB by pressing "0" anywhere in the script;

This standard set of services is determined by the scenario, which may be supplemented or expanded by means of opportunities Voicelant.

Voice fax autoinformer (VAI).

Voice fax autoinformer (VAI) - it is a voicemail box on the contrary, works on the principle: to listen for any possible caller, record - only for the owner who knows the password.

The caller can to dial the number RAI, listen to the message or receive a fax. Record messages in VAI performed by the owner after entering the password.

After the authorization, holder has at disposal a set of control codes VAI, a similar set of codes VMB.

VAI has all features of the advanced IVR.

Automatic outbound notification of subscribers (Voice Automatic Notification-VAN).

Automatic notification - this is phone calls to subscribers from a specific list of telephone numbers with the transfer of the required message (for example, the amount owed for the phone).

Automatic notification produced in some time as scheduled. At the same time can work a lot of VANs, each of whom works on his scenario and schedule, calls out its subscribers and gives away them different messages.

All VANs shares allocated to them a set of channels. Channels to the set shall appoint an administrator (from 1 to max number of channels).

Set control codes VAN, similar sets owner VAI (VMB).

In the supply Voicelant-IVR includes examples of VANs:

  • Routing facsimile documents.
  • Delivery of messages with the possibility of communication with the operator.

All VANs work on their scenarios, with examples which are easy to implement other algorithms notification, which would be best to meet your needs.
